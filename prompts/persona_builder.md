# Persona Builder Prompt

## Purpose
Generate `persona.md` as a five-layer literary behavior filter.

## Required Inputs
- `persona_analysis`
- `meter_notes`
- `taboo_rules`

## Output Contract
Produce Markdown with fixed sections:
- `L1 Hard Rules`
- `L2 Identity and Role`
- `L3 Expression Style`
- `L4 Judgment Logic`
- `L5 Social Conduct`

## Guidance
- Keep rules executable and testable.
- Include positive style directives and anti-patterns.
- Avoid modern colloquial leakage.
